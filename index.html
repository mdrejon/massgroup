Welcome to Mass Group

Mass group was founded as a partnership firm in 2007.

Mass group is a multi-disciplinary company, has been involved in consultancy services, real estate sector, construction sector, media(online and electronic media),food sector, supply chain and logistic services, Since its inception, the group has been providing services to the government,privete and corporate sectors. The hall marks of Mass Group has been our dedication to serving each of our clients individual needs. We believe the numerous awards we have received and our repeat clients serve as testimony to the success of our approach.

Brief of Mass Group

Our company history takes back to 2005 when we started as a consultancy firm named SMS consultancy on sugandha R/A in Panchlaish, Chittagong. In 2015 we formed a partnership and relocated the business to “Ramna Trade Center, CDA Avenue, Muradpur, Chittagong”. During this time, the firm concentrated on small office buildings and store fronts, projects typical of the era.

Over the last few years the company grew taking a more challenging and varied projects. We intend to be a one-stop solution for all year civil construction and interior requirement.
